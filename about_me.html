About Me

I am a graduate of the Department of Product & Systems Design Engineering of the University of Aegean (2015 - 2022), specializing in the Design of Interactive Systems.

I have worked as a Unity developer for the Cultural Technology Lab of the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications, Nationali Kapodistrian University of Athens (January - September 2023). In my time there, I assisted in the development of culture and museum oriented gaming experiences.

I have knowledge of several technologies, with the most extensive one being of the Unity Engine, which I've acquired through my professional work, as well as my involvevment with academic and personal projects.

My interest lies mostly within games, especially educational ones. I am also keen with experimenting with new technologies.

You can contact me through mail, and my Linkedin profile.
