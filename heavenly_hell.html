Heavenly Hell

This game was created for the Global Game Jam 2022, based on the theme "Duality". This puzzle platformer's main idea is that the levels are torn between Heaven and Hell, and the player must constantly switch from one dimention to another in order to traverse through the level and avoid the obstacles.


Co - creators:

Simonian (Pixel Art - Animation), Eva (Pixel Art), Step 9(Music & Sounds)
